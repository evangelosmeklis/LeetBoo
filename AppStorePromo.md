# LeetBoo App Store Promo

## App Store Description
LeetBoo is your daily LeetCode companion for building consistent interview prep habits and earning Leetcoins as you go. Track your daily check‑ins, crush daily challenges, and stay motivated with progress insights, streaks, and contest reminders. Whether you are preparing for your first technical interview or sharpening your competitive programming skills, LeetBoo helps you stay accountable and celebrate progress.

Key highlights:
- Daily check‑ins and challenge tracking to build momentum
- Rolling streaks to encourage consistency
- Weekly contest and Lucky Monday reminders
- Progress snapshots that show how far you are from your goal
- Simple, focused design built for daily use

Start building a lasting LeetCode routine with LeetBoo — the faster, friendlier way to stay on track.
