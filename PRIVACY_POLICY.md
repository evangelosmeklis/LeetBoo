# Privacy Policy for LeetBoo

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

LeetBoo ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What Data We Collect

LeetBoo collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Coin Tracking Data**: Your current coin count and target coin goals
- **Activity Preferences**: Which activities you have enabled (daily check-ins, daily problems, weekly luck)
- **Activity History**: Log of activities you've completed, including dates and activity types
- **Notification Settings**: Your preferred notification times and frequency preferences
- **Mission Completion**: Records of completed one-time and weekly missions
- **App Preferences**: Custom monthly rate settings and dismissed banner information

### How We Store Data

All data collected by LeetBoo is stored **locally on your device** using iOS UserDefaults. We do not transmit, upload, or sync your data to any external servers, cloud services, or third-party platforms.

## Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

**We do not share your data with anyone.**

LeetBoo does not:
- Share data with third-party services
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements
- Collect personal information
- Require user accounts or login
- Transmit data over the internet
- Use cloud storage or backup services

## Permissions

### Notification Permission

LeetBoo requests permission to send you local notifications to remind you about:
- Daily check-ins
- Daily problem reminders
- Weekly luck bonuses
- Contest reminders (if enabled)

These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not require internet connectivity or external services. You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data is protected by your device's security features
- Data is not accessible to us or any third parties
- If you delete the app, all data is removed from your device
- We recommend using your device's built-in backup features if you want to preserve your data

## Children's Privacy

LeetBoo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal information from any users, this app is safe for users of all ages.

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **View Your Data**: All data is visible within the app
- **Modify Your Data**: You can update coin counts, targets, and settings at any time
- **Delete Your Data**: You can reset all data through the app settings, or delete the app to remove all data

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the App Store listing or your preferred method of communication.

## Summary

**In short**: LeetBoo stores all your data locally on your device. We don't track you, we don't share your data, and we don't use any third-party services. Your privacy is fully protected because your data never leaves your device.

---

*This privacy policy applies to LeetBoo version 1.1 and later.*
